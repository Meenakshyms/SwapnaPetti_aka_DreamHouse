# SWAPNA PETTI (Dream Box)

## Product Specification v1.0

### Product Name

Swapna Petti

### Meaning

Swapna = Dream

Petti = Box

### Tagline

"സ്വപ്നങ്ങൾക്ക് സ്വന്തം പെട്ടി"

(A home for your dreams.)

---

# Product Vision

Swapna Petti is a digital dream box where people can:

* Store dreams
* Create bucket lists
* Track goals
* Save memories
* Maintain a diary
* Connect with friends
* Join communities
* Celebrate achievements
* Build a personal story over time

The platform combines:

* Bucket List App
* Journal App
* Memory Keeper
* Productivity Tool
* Social Platform

into a single experience.
